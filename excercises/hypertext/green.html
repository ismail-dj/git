Getting into Guitar

I finally managed to get a guitar when my sister bought me an acoustic guitar soon after I finished high school.
I learned a lot of the basics but didn't really get into guitar until I bought myself an electric a year later when I then learned many of my favorite songs
I was getting more into stuff like shoegaze and dream pop bands such as My Bloody Valentine or Lush which relied on guitar effects
Eventually I started to take guitar lessons which improved my skills immensely. I also started to learn music theory which helped my skill
Since I learned some theory I was able to actually make my own songs unlike before.
